The brief:
In January of 2020, I joined the Cantec Courier project. It was a wonderful chance for me as a junior UI/UX designer to be able to start a large project from the beginning. It took me approximately 3 months to come up with concepts, investigate rivals, define target users, create a business plan, colour scheme, and wireframe.

We want to establish a courier service to link with Advesa's CRM platform in order to eliminate the complexity for clients and speed up their sales process. The Cantec courier service will be an independent website in the first phase. A shipment can be booked by store owners that use Breadstack CRM.

In the second phase, this service will be integrated into Woocommerce stores as a delivery method. In the checkout stage, retail shoppers can choose from the cart.

ii. Strategy

BOD decisions

Cantec Couriers 3rd party portal allows a business that is not currently using Breadstack CRM to be able to access fast local delivery service provided by Cantec Couriers.

Product team setup

Project Manager, UI/UX designer team and Developers.

iii. Problem/Opportunity

Market Problem

Canada has a great delivery infrastructure due to its many highways, paved roads and bridges. Most slow-downs will occur due to winter weather and customs.

Customs handling times
In general, customs may only take a few days to a week to receive the shipments. However, customs can sometimes hold packages up to a few weeks or a month. There are many cases, the customers place orders. They have not known that customs can delay their packages from the expected delivery date.

Snow, sleet, and other weather conditions
Winters are brutal in Canada. The courier services have to prepare for slowdowns caused by snow and sleet on the road in the winter. They usually predict and plan to let customers know ahead of time that package delivery might be delayed because of weather conditions.

Cost to run shipping services.
The owner needs to manage expensive warehouses, complicated logistics operations, or hire and train labour.

User Problem

With the rise of eCommerce, more customers than ever before are making purchases online. And since Canada shares a border with the US, many US companies often include Canada in their marketing.

The delivery address was wrong or incomplete
The shipper delivered the items as wrong addressed and he has to make every attempt to correct any addressing issues.

Track status shows as Delivered
If the Track status of the customer parcel shows as Delivered, but the item has not been received.

The item has not arrived by the expected date
The tracking system is not working well. It has not to update immediately to the customer when they needed.

The package that the customer sent within Canada arrived damaged
Whether the customer package included the insurance coverage? Or how can they claim to recoup the postage and the insured value?

New features or Enhancements

The Cantec Courier is an enhancement in delivery service in Canada.

New Cantec - a local delivery service that makes the process of shipment easier and quicker. The more powerful platform focuses on customer experience from the beginning till they receive their parcel perfectly. Otherwise, Cantec still adds somehow to existing functionality.

iv. Develop Solution

Market Problem

Indeed, e-commerce orders have resulted in higher than normal small parcel volumes. For example, Canada Post issued an advisory stating, “Canadians should anticipate parcel delays for the foreseeable future, even as Canada Post delivers at record levels.” On May 19, Canada Post hit an all-time, one-day record with 2.1 million parcels delivered to Canadians. That is about three times the norm for this time of year according to the postal operator.

In Canada, some companies provide same-day delivery services. For example big corporate FedEx, UPS... with a professional staff, firmly quality control of each stage in the whole procedure.

In addition, FedEx and UPS offer alternative pickup locations at their FedEx Ship Centers and UPS Stores locations across Canada.

To meet same-day expectations, a growing number of store-front retailers offer curbside pickup and Buy Online Pick Up in Stores (BOPIS).

We choose to focus on some points at the beginning.

  • Local same-day courier service in the Lower Mainland.
  • Express deliveries Door to Door personnel.
  • Competitive pricing.
  • Good quality driver.
  • Invest in user experience App/Website.
  • Reliable courier company.
  • Great customer support 24/24.

20 second Gut test

Conduct Gut testing helped me to get more insight into the feeling of users when they see a delivery website. From that, I could start with general design aesthetics.
