Entrade X

i. Introduction

Company: Dai Nam Securities Joint Stock Company

My role:

  • Competitor Research
  • Persona
  • User Journey
  • Information Architecture
  • UX/UI Design

Design for: Website

Learn more: dnse.com.vn

The context:
By the end of 2020, I've chosen to change jobs. Despite the fact that the covid epidemic is spreading, I'm seeing an increase in people investing in the stock and financial markets. As a result, I accepted the employment offer from the Stock company in early 2021. I started researching and building Entrade X - Underlying Securities Web Platform in March 2021.

Because the trading application has already been developed and implemented. Our Product Owner's idea is to build two platforms that cater to two distinct groups of individuals. Brokers have priority on the Web, while retail users have priority on the App.

The trading site's purpose is to improve the high-value transaction trading experience. In 2021, it will also increase revenue by boosting the number of new users who make at least one purchase on the site. The first release is scheduled for July 2021. It was tested by 10 to 20 internal users, the majority of whom are brokers.

ii. Strategy

Company vision and mission

Vision: To become Vietnam's most valuable stock firm.

Mission: To design a simple platform that allows everyone in Vietnam to begin stock trading with ease.

Business model

We chose B2C and Freemium as our business models.
B2C - Create a platform that allows retail consumers to quickly create an account and buy and sell stocks on their own.
Freemium - With brokers, we offer more powerful consulting services. Users that want to update their assets to maximize their profit can do so with margin products.

BOD decisions

  • Right on target, it will be able to fit into the finance industry. At the time, serve the wants and deficiencies.
  • In comparison to existing items on the market, the product must provide a positive customer experience.
  • In the two years since the product's release, the number of new users has increased by one million.
  • Reduce the acquisition cost from 300.000đ to under 100.000đ.
  • It's best to keep the internal IT team small (about 3-6 members per specific team).
  • Adhere to Agile methodology, which enables teams to deliver value to consumers faster and with fewer difficulties.
  • The CEO wants the entire client journey to be automated in the long run. From the initial approach to long-term client care and education on new features.

Product team setup

Product Owner, UI/UX Designer, Business Analyst, Developers.

iii. Problem/Opportunity

Market Problem

When inflation rises and the economy does not recover as projected, bank bad debts rise dramatically. Transparency and capital mobilization of the exchange's listed companies. The process of opening a securities account is lengthy, with numerous procedures and documentation to complete. Vietnamese investors prioritize investment in real estate, gold and then the stock market.

User Problem

Overwhelmed by information on the internet. Is not aware of the finance market. Because of the crash and reports of people losing all of their assets. The user is skeptical about the stock market.

New features or Enhancements

Entrade X is a product that performs similarly to other products in the market. However, the user experience has improved.

iv. Develop Solution

UX Research

I did some market research to understand and determine if a new service or product is viable in the present market.

User Interview

Internal users were interviewed. User retail and user broker are included. The same trading platform will be used, however, different functions will be offered to enhance the experience.

UI Design

Hi-fi Design

After the Product Owner confirms the flow. The Hi-Fi design will be uploaded to Zeplin. I will add comments to describe the details for developers.

Prototype

The first prototype was then created based on the insights from the research and competitor analysis. We tested and show demo flow for stakeholders easier to adapt and get the idea.

v. Implement

Working process with devs

Initially, the corporation did not divide its employees into tiny groups. On a single App project, people collaborated in a large group. Since my arrival, we've formed a new web team. However, the developers have no concept of how to communicate effectively between the designer and the BA. We had disagreements over altering the designs, a mismatch with the BA's demand, and the designer's failure to communicate the ideas to the developers.

By getting the information in team group chat, all those involved in the implementation of the designs (devs, BA, etc) can be part of a smooth hand-off process.By getting the information in team group chat, all those involved in the implementation of the designs (devs, BA, etc) can be part of a smooth hand-off process. Each team member is focused on their tasks instead of spending time to find the necessary specs (want & need), therefore reducing the overall back-and-forths within the implementation process.

I requested that the devs and BA not use the chat channel to connect to me privately. We should use Jira and Zeplin to make comments so that everyone on the team understands what's going on. Furthermore, I make an effort to engage with team members more frequently via group chat, which is the quickest and easiest way to contact them.

vi. Testing

Quality Assurance

  • Business testing will be handled by Business Analysis. API tests the formula, for example, and creates feature files for developers.
  • Usability testing, UI testing, and functional testing will all be done by UI/UX design.
  • Brokers, Customer Support representatives, and other internal staff members also test and report any bugs.
  • I frequently share end-user comments or my point of view with BA, Broker members for discussion. It's vital to encourage members to connect and share knowledge. Each team actively inform others if there is any issue happening it would be great.

Internal Test

I conducted UI and functional testing with early adopter users, who are brokers, in the first phase. They were using the trading web MVP version, which had only the most basic functions. Login, logout, alter dark/light theme, track watchlist, deal book, portfolio, and buy/sell tickers are just a few examples.

I gathered user perceptions and expectations, as well as the challenges they face in the actual world. I realized that the development team's thinking and end-user behaviour were vastly different. As a result, while new features are being developed, I've chosen to ask and listen to users more. Taking notes and organizing them in one space, such as the Notion platform, is also a fantastic idea. It makes it easier for me to keep track of and access the information when I need it.

vii. Measure Impact

KPI

  • The biggest goal is to improve the registration procedure.
    How do users quickly start using Entrade X in a snap?
  • Customers' satisfaction is measured.
  • Get insights from users' difficulties and concerns through real-life case studies every day.

Traders appear to prefer using the Entrade X App over the Trading website to complete deals.

Besides, marketing channels such as Facebook, Tiktok, Telegram, and Zalo. To attract new users, we also develop a Refer-a-Friend program. According to the data, the majority of new users were born in 2002. They are young, so having the opportunity to educate them about the stock and finance markets could be a good sign for us. But, because they are unfamiliar with the stock, they will drop and forget about Entrade X.

Users must complete three steps to finish the registration process. Since more than 80% of users do not want to spend time uploading their papers, Entrade X should improve this procedure to increase the number of users who complete the task.

Retail consumers have a higher total transaction value than Premium users who use the Broker service. It may be a positive sign to continue improving the trading experience as well as customer service.

Conclusion

As a UI/UX Designer, I should never assume that something will work for my users; instead, I should validate all designs as much as possible by testing them with the target audience.

viii. Referral

Since I joined the company, I was directly assigned to build the trading website. My CEO told me that he thinks I'll do fantastic at it. Together with me, he wants to learn about product design and customer experience. Over ten years of finance experience, he worked with a variety of domestic and international trading products. The CEO agreed that great products aren't just about how many features they offer; they're also about how satisfied customers learn and grow as a result of using the product.

When my design was mentioned in the first demo, the internal members were excited. They noticed that the former website version's details, flow, and features were all organized differently. That's how UX plays a vital role in developing the product.

Every day, our customer support team collects feedback from customers via several sources (FB, Zalo, Mail, Telegram). They informed the Web team that the feedback had been positive. Customers are immediately fascinated with the new web trading version.
